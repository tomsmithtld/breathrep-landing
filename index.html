Why I built this

I do Wim Hof every morning. Used to just use a YouTube video but wanted something that tracked my retention times and showed progress over weeks.

Most breathing apps are too relaxation-focused. Soft sounds, wellness vibes. I wanted something more like a training tool. Set the protocol, press go, get your reps in.

BreathRep is the breathing app I wanted to exist. Performance-focused, precise timing, and it stays out of your way.
